project gym



service :

Web development - fitness

front-end

Vue.js

back-end

Node.js Express.js

This is my pass through project for my engineer degree on my University. I wanted to develop something that could be really useful, especially for me because fitness area is one of my hobbies. There are four main areas there. First of them is 'training' - you can look for some exercises inspirations. When you view details of particular training you just learning how to do it. 'Diet' is second part of project gym. Similar to 'training' there are some meals that you can prepare with guide and macroelements. It is really important if you want to eat well. Search bar, history of searching and favorites are included to both of areas. You can also edit, delete and add meals and exercises. Third part is 'products'. There you can carry on your daily macroelements and see details of particular products from grocery stores. Searching and favorites are also included there. Fourth are is 'calculators'. There you can type some data and application will show you results, for an example you can calculate your body mass index, one rep max etc.
